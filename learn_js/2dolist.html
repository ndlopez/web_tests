Establish a routine

When it comes to productivity, nothing beats a well-established routine:

  1. Get up early and exercise to get your body going
  2. Plan for your day ahead
  3. Have a few hours of "focus time"
  4. Plan for the next day
  5. Wrap up work and disconnect
  6. Go to sleep early
  7. Repeat

it sounds kinda boring, but establishing a routine like that will free up time for the fun things you want to do.
